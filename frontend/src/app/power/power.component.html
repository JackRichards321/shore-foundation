Individual donors

Our power generators are generous donors that personally support the mission of The Shore Foundation with a financial contribution.

A generator converts one form of energy into another, much like our donors who's contributions become refurbished computers for people in-need. The Power Generators giving group provides annual financial contributions in support of The Shore Foundation.

Generators are a reliable power source, and the same is true for this giving group. We appreciate their steadfast support and the energy they provide to our mission.
