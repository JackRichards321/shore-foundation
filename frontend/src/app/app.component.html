THE SHORE FOUNDATION, 52 MARWAY CIR, ROCHESTER, NY, UNITED STATES

The Shore Foundation provides access to technology for low-income individuals and families in our community. Over the last 3 years The Shore Foundation has donated over 1,000 refurbished computers to non-profit organizations and schools in Upstate New York.
