Support The Shore Foundation

Your support of The Shore Foundation directly impacts our community and people in-need who lack access to technology. We appreciate the individuals, companies, foundations and organizations that assist in our efforts.

Personal donations of any amount are greatly appreciated. Your contribution funds the refurbishment of a computer and that could be the first computer, ever, for someone. Thank you for believing in a connected community.

Corporate support

Our CPU (Community Partner Union) is made of companies and foundations that financially support The Shore Foundation.

Individual donors

Our Power Generators are individuals who provide annual financial contributions that support The Shore Foundation's mission and impact on the digital divide.

In-kind contributions

Our cloud support is made of companies and individuals that support The Shore Foundation through in-kind donations and volunteer efforts.
