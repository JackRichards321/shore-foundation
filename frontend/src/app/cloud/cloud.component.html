In-kind supporters

Our cloud support is made of companies and individuals that support The Shore Foundation through in-kind donations and volunteer efforts.

Much like a cloud is overhead, in-kind contributions greatly impact The Shore Foundation and offset our overhead expenses. And in the digital world, resources are often stored in "the cloud."

These Cloud Support in-kind contributors provide valuable resources of technology, tools, time and talent to help us fulfill our mission at The Shore Foundation.
