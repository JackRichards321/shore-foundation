Reuse

Our Computer Refurbishment Process:

Corporations like MARS Inc. send us their used laptops, desktops, and peripherals, and we give them a new life and a good home.

Step 1: Apply barcode asset tag and scan into system for tracking through the process.

Step 2: Data wipe. Plug computer into our network software and receive certificate of destruction.

Step 3: Install a Microsoft licensed Windows 10 package which includes built-in essential software such as antivirus, Open Office, and Adobe PDF reader.

Step 4: Test supporting equipment for working functionality: charger, keyboard, mouse, and monitor.

Step 5: Physically clean computer and donate it to a local school or non-profit!
