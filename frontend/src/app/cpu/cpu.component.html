Corporate supporters

Our CPU (Community Partner Union) is made of companies and foundations that financially support The Shore Foundation and believe in a connected community.

A CPU is often considered the brain of the computer, but our CPU is the heart of The Shore Foundation. These members show their commitment to our mission through financial contributions.

Community Partner Union (CPU) members contribute annually through their company or foundation to cover program expenses to refurbish technology for people in need.

Impact the digital divide in your community

Thank you to our generous corporate supporters and foundations (full listing to come soon).

CPU members can choose the non-profit organization(s) who will receive refurbished computers/laptops from your contribution. Computer quantity corresponds with the different levels of support but ranges from 15 to 100 computers annually.

CPU members will also receive a formal donation acknowledgement letter, metallic plaque on the CPU recognition wall, sign with company logo in our Warehouse, name and logo on this webpage, invitations to CPU member-only receptions/networking events, and feature(s) on our social media channels with company tagged mention.

Full CPU program information is available by contacting our Executive Director. This includes the financial contribution amount tiers and corresponding recognition.

Interested in joining? Please contact our Executive Director to discuss your interests and she can discuss the tiers and community impact for each.

Thank you for believing in a connected community.
